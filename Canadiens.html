The Montreal Canadiens

Founded in 1909, the Canadiens are the longest continuously operating professional ice hockey team worldwide.

The canadiens are also an orignal 6 team. The Canadiens have won the Stanley Cup more times than any other franchise. They have won 24 Stanley Cups, 23 of them since the founding of the NHL and 22 of them since 1927. The canadiens and the Bruins are big rivals. love to root against them.
